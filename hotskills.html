Heart of Thorns Build


This build is the current metagame build to date.
This does require the Heart of Thorns expansion as this build requires the Daredevil specialization.
With high DPS through a two-handed weapon, a staff, and high mobility through evades and dodges, use this to clear your way through Maguuma Jungle!
It is incredibly satisfying to play but it took a bit of a learning curve as I had been running around Guild Wars 2 with my dungeon build for so long that I was shocked when I had to be more active in keeping an eye on my HP.


Extra Notes

This build will not gain anything from entering stealth (no condi clease or regenration) but use it as a moment's respite to kite around the battle. This is an incredibly high DPS build so watch your numbers go up and hit everything with what you got!
