General PvE Build


This is a really fun build to run around Guild Wars 2 outside of Heart of Thorns expansion. It's got great mobility through dodges and evasion and boon stealing. While it's an old guide, I still turn to it when I get bored of running around with my dungeon build.


Extra Notes

Where this shines is the usage of Infiltrator's Strike (which is Sword 2) through exploiting the environment. And the use of Cloak and Dagger to stealth away.
Really fun to use, give it a try!
