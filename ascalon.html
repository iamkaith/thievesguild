Introduction to Ascalonian Catacombs

Ascalonian Catacombs is the first dungeon you unlock in Guild Wars 2. This dungeon has a story mode which requires you to be level 30 before entering and it also has an explorable mode which requires you to be level 35.

It is highly recommended to go with a full party of 5 if you are attempting this dungeon with a party member whose character is below level 80.
