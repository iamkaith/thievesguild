Welcome to Thieves Guild!


The focus of Thieves Guild is to provide a thief focused perspective in the Guild Wars 2 world. There are a lot of general purpose guides out on the web, plenty of forum discussion, but not a website dedicated to a single class. That's what I hope to achieve with this website.

Main topics that will be discussed are:

  • Various Thief Skill Builds and Equipment
  • Dungeons
  • PvE content
.

What is Guild Wars 2?


Guild Wars 2 is an online free-to-play MMORPG . The main content of the game is free-to-play and there is currently 1 expansion pack, Heart of Thorns, which is availble to purchase and unlock new content.
