Explorable Mode Guide

Dungeon Walkthrough: Tzark's Path


Tomb of Lovers

Keep at least 1 collector working until the progress bar his 100%. Destroy all the burrows that spawn so the hatchlings don't destroy the collectors.


Closed gate door

Destroy the 4 burrows to open the door.


Tunnels to Colossus Rumblus

The tunnels will split 3-ways, pick any path and fight your way through. Make sure to go slowly and do it as a team so everyone survives.


Colossus Rumblus

During this boss fight rocks will fall from the ceiling, the NPC will cast a shield once the rocks start falling so make sure you get in there to avoid getting hit. Make sure the NPC survives so kite Colossus away from the NPC and do as much DPS as you can.
Attack his butt and stay away from his face. It is highly recommended to range in this fight so you can run quickly to the shield if the rocks start falling.


Dungeon Complete

Congratulations! You have completed the dungeon!
