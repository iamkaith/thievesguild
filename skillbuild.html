What are skill builds?


Skills are abilities you activate in-game to perform an action. A skill build is a a set of specific skills and specialization traits optimized for the environment the player will be in.

This doesn't necessarily mean every time you change a map, go into a different area, that you will have to change your build. You may only need to change it when you enter a dungeon, a fractal, or to suit your party.

You don't have to change your build all the time, pick the one that is right for you and your play style. Below are some links to builds I currently use in different areas of Guild Wars.

What are equipment sets?


Each weapon in Guild Wars 2 has different skills and abilities. For a thief you have the option of using: swords, daggers, pistols, shortbow and if you have unlocked the Daredevil specialization: a staff.

Experiment to see which weapons suit your fancy!
